ğŸ” CoinTrack Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Key Summary: CoinTrack stores your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal financial data.

1. Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple ID or Google, we only receive your display name and email address for identification purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve user experience.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your financial transaction records
  • Your voice recordings
  • Your diary content
  • Your location information

2. Data Storage

All your expense records, diary entries, and personal settings are stored locally on your device. We use iOS system security mechanisms to protect your data.

3. Voice Features

CoinTrack uses Apple's Speech Recognition service to process your voice input. Voice data is processed by Apple, and we do not store or transmit your voice recordings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In: For account authentication
  • Google Sign In: For account authentication
  • Apple Speech Recognition: For speech-to-text

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data stored locally on device
  • iOS system-level encryption
  • No transmission of sensitive data over network

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Manage your account bindings in settings
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

CoinTrack is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified through in-app notifications.
